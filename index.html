MATH MEETS MAGIC

Think of any card in a regular 52 card deck

Remember this card and prepare for a little math

You are going to take the value of your card and multiply it by 2

  • Aces = 1
  • 2-10 remain the same
  • Kings = 13
  • Queens = 12
  • Jacks = 11

Now add 3 to that new number

Muliply it by 5
